1. Download and install https://fabricmc.net/use/installer/ in your launcher. Choose version 1.17.1.

2. Download BubbleClient (this is the modpack)

3. Extract it into your %APPDATA%/.minecraft/mods/ folder. Make sure nothing else is inside this folder!

4. Download configs and put them inside %APPDATA%/.minecraft/config/ folder, just like in step 3.

5. Increase RAM for fabric loader in your launcher ( Instruction )

6. Enjoy playing!
